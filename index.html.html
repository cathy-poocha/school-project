Conservation Efforts

There are many organizations and initiatives working to protect the coral reefs of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. We can protect coral reefs by reducing greenhouse gas emissions, establishing marine protected areas and promoting sustainable practices. The coral reefs of Andaman is being protected by creating marine protected areas (MPAs).
